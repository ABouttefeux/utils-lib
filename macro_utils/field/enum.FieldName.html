pub fn from_field_ref(field: &Field) -> Self

Get the way to access the field from a Field. It clones only field.ident and therefore less resource intensive than cloning the whole field in Self::from_field using Self::from_field(field.clone()).

source

pub fn from_field(field: Field) -> Self

Get the way to access the field from a Field. If you want to reuse the field afterward use Self::from_field_ref instead.
